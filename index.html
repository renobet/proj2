About Us

Woodside Smile Dental began as a family dental group devoted to comprehensive care with an emphasis on prevention. We are a team of general, implant and cosmetic dentists as well as an orthodontist and a periodontist. We take great pride in providing the highest quality dental care to our patients.

Expertise in dentistry

As leaders in the evolving fields of dentistry, from implantology through cosmetic dentistry, we help our patients achieve their goals. Whether a simple cleaning, replacing a filling or transforming a smile, we are prepared to see you through the process.
